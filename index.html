About Me

Hi, I’m Zack Firestone, a woodworker based in Niceville, Florida. I specialize in handcrafted wooden American flags — each made with care, precision, and a deep respect for our country’s heritage. Every flag I build tells a story of craftsmanship, patriotism, and purpose. Whether it’s for your home, your department, or as a meaningful gift, each piece is made to last and inspire.

The Design Process

Each wooden American flag is handcrafted from start to finish using quality wood. Every stripe and star is carefully measured, cut, and sanded by hand to preserve the authentic proportions and spirit of the flag.

I torch each piece to bring out the natural grain, stain it to achieve bold and timeless color, and finish with a protective coat for durability — creating a rugged, weathered look that embodies freedom and resilience.
